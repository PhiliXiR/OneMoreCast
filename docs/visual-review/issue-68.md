# Issue 68 — Cast-to-waiting water reactions

`issue-68-cast-entry.png` captures the cast-entry reaction: a brief central
splash highlight followed by two small, expanding surface rings centered on the
terminal tackle. `issue-68-waiting-lure.png` captures the gentler lure pulse
that remains once the cast has settled.

Both reactions are rendered by `LakeSurface`; the lure, hook, target marker,
and fishing-line overlay remain outside the effect layer for readability.
